About Me

I was raised outside of Boston, MA. I graduated from Assumption College with a B.A. in Philosphy in 2000. After school I worked as a counselor for teenage girls, and then in accounts payable. At that time, I wanted to explore the country and find a more fullfilling career that I was passionate about.

I moved across country and graduated from Arizona Culinary Insitute in 2005. There wasn't much opportunity in Phoenix so I moved to Los Angeles shortly after. Since then, I have worked as a pastry chef in various restaurants and catering companies such as Along Came Mary and Intercontinental Hotel. I now specialize in vegan baking when I decided that I wanted to meet the demands of healthy eaters, myself included, in Los Angeles.

When the Corona Virus hit, I found myself unemployed and in need of a new career so I am teaching myself front end web design. It has also left me with plenty of time to persue my hobbies such as hiking, yoga, reading, watching movies, paint by numers and bird watching. My favorite types of books and movies are mystery and horror especially if they have a culinary theme. Before the pandemic hit I also played in a dodgeball league and rode bikes around the city with friends.
